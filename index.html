About Me

Hi there! I'm Natalie Garcia. I grew up in Peachtree City, Georgia. I have spent the last four years at the University of Georgia pursuing a degree in Entertainment and Media Studies. I am graduating in Summer of 2020 with a Bachelor of Arts, a Film studies minor, and a New Media Certificate. Film and television will always have my heart, but I enjoy veiwing them more than I do creating them. I am currently working to become a Full-Stack Web Developer through the Georgia Tech Coding Bootcamp Program. When I first starting coding it just made sense to me and I loved trying to fit the pieces together to make a code work. I have never felt like I was passionate enough for the careers I was pursing until I began coding. I would like to pursue a career in Web Development so I can have a career I love, that consistently challenges me, and I will continually learn from.

When I am not staring at a computer screen, I love to bake, workout, and binge watch TV. I have a 2 year-old boxer named Henley, and she is the goodest girl in the world! When it comes to organizing, I am your girl! I also enjoy refurbishing furniture and plan to build my skills in woodworking as a hobby.
